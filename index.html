As a engineering student, The Diamond could probably be the most important space for us, and we would spend most of our time in this building. The Diamond was completed in 2015, composed of 6 floors and various teaching and practicing areas for engineering student. All of lecture theaters are on the ground floor and you can enter any of these theaters without a university ID card, which means every citizen in the Sheffield can have all of university lectures at the Diamond. You will need a university card to enter the studying space from 1st floor to 4th floor, but these floors are open to all of students in the university of Sheffield, not only the engineering students. All of students can use computer rooms and the library area to grasp the knowledge, and have a incredible experience in this amazing building.
